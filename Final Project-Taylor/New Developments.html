New Developments
Since technology is always changing that means that there is always new technology being made. New developments include Robot dexterity, New-Wave Nuclear Power, Gut probes in a pill, and even custom cancer vaccines.
Robot Dexterity
Robot dexterity is the ability of robots to "pick up a component on an assembly line with amazing percision and without ever getting bored".
New-Wave Nuclear Power
New-Wave Nuclear Power is a new safer and cheaper source of power. Among them are generation IV fission reactors, an evolution of traditional designs.
Gut Probes
What the gut probes do is let the patient swallow a device that is capable of dpture detailed pictures of the gut without anesthesia. It works even with babies and children.
Custom Cancer Vaccines
Custom cancer vaccines incite the body's natural defenses to destroy only cancer cells by identifying the mutations in the tumor. Scientists are close to releasing the first personalized cancer vaccine.
